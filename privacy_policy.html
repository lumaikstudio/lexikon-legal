Privacy Policy for Lexikon

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Lexikon ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application Lexikon (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Information you provide: When you use Lexikon, you may provide a display name which is stored locally on your device and in our database for leaderboard purposes.

Automatically collected information: We collect anonymous usage data including game progress, scores, and performance statistics. This data is stored locally on your device and may be synced to our servers.

Device information: We may collect device identifiers for the purpose of providing our services and tracking game progress across sessions.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve the App
- Display your scores on the leaderboard
- Save your game progress
- Send you notifications about daily rewards (if enabled)
- Analyze usage patterns to improve gameplay

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Firebase (Google): We use Firebase for data storage, authentication, and analytics. Firebase may collect and process data according to Google's Privacy Policy available at https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google AdMob: We use AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings. AdMob's privacy policy is available at https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google Play Billing: In-app purchases are processed through Google Play. Google's payment privacy policy applies to all transactions.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your game data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage and may be backed up to our Firebase database. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access.

5. Children's Privacy

Lexikon is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request deletion of your data using the "Reset Progress" option in Settings
- Opt out of personalized advertisements through your device settings
- Disable notifications through the App settings

7. Data Retention

We retain your game data for as long as you use the App. You can delete your data at any time using the "Reset Progress" option in the Settings screen.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this policy.
